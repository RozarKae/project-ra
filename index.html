Rozar & Arifa

Chapter I

Our Story

Where It All Began

It started with a simple promise—a photoshoot to capture a moment. We were two different worlds: Rozar, with his calm and logical architecture, and Arifa, with her vibrant and emotional colors. We didn't expect the camera to capture a connection that would slowly redraw our lives.

From the misty heights of Kodaikanal to quiet beach walks at sunset, we found a comfortable silence. It wasn't loud, and it wasn't rushed. We shared conversations that felt like returning home, and realized that our different paths were mapping the exact same destination.

With the blessing of our families, we now stand ready. We have waited for this day, through patience and prayer. We invite you to be there as we exchange our vows and begin the journey of a lifetime under the grace of the Almighty.

"And We created you in pairs." Surah An-Naba 78:8
