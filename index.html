Chapter I

Our Story

Where It All Began

It began with two completely opposite worlds colliding—one quiet and content in silence, the other full of words and restless energy. We crossed paths when a simple request was made for our first picture together, followed by a bold promise: "One day, I'll do a proper photoshoot for you." A promise that we both somehow let slip away, only for it to become the running joke that tied us together.

From the cool breezes of Kodaikanal to the endless horizons of beach walks, we found our rhythm in between the contrasts. There were quiet compromises, differences that made us laugh, and challenges that tested our resolve. Yet, through every obstacle and distance, we realized that our paths didn't just cross; they ran parallel towards the same destination.

With our families' warm blessings, our promise grew into a lifelong commitment at our engagement. Now, after months of waiting, our journey brings us here. As we stand on the threshold of our wedding, we invite you to be part of the moment our single steps become a shared lifetime.
